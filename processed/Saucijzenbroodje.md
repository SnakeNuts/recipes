# Saucijzenbroodje

A super simple and delicious snack!

## Ingredients

- 500g beef mince
- 1 tbsp mustard + extra
- 1 tbsp sambal
- 1-2 tsp onion powder
- 1-2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Pinch of nutmeg
- 1 egg
- 75-150g cheese
- Big splash kecap manis
- Splash worcestershire sauce
- Panko bread crumbs
- 10-12 puff pastries

## Method

1. Mix the beef with all the ingredients and add enough breadcrumbs to make a strong mixture.

2. Make 10-12 sausages and set aside.

3. Brush the puff pastry with mustard and wrap the sausages into them. Brush with egg and bake for 15-20 minutes at 200-220°C.
