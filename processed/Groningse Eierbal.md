# Groningse Eierbal

A famous Groningse treat!

## Ingredients

### Roux Base
- 110g flour
- 60g butter
- 2 tsp curry
- 2 tsp parsley
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp dijon mustard
- 425ml veggie stock
- 8 eggs

### Coat
- 200g flour
- 6 eggs
- 200g breadcrumbs

## Method

1. Sieve the flour and mix with the curry, mustard, parsley and salt.

2. Melt the butter in a pan and go in with the flour. Fry down for a few minutes.

3. Slowly add the cold vegetable stock while whisking constantly.

4. Layer into a tray and let cooldown for 20 minutes before putting it in the freezer for 2 hours to stiffen up.

5. Boil the eggs for 5-6 minutes and immediately cool down in an ice bath. Peel and let cool down completely.

6. Now divide the roux in 8 pieces and flatten out so they can be wrapped around the eggs.

7. Coat the eggs in flour, beaten eggs and bread crumbs.

8. Let them stiffen up in the freezer for a minimum of 8 hours.

9. Deep fry the eggs for 5-6 minutes at 160-170°C.
