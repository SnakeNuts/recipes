# Beef Stew Cottage Pie

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 800g stew/casserole beef pieces
- 1 onion
- 1 medium leek
- 25g butter
- 300g chestnut mushrooms
- 3-4 medium carrots
- 2 tbsp plain flour
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tbsp garlic granules
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 rich beef stock
- 300ml red wine
- Boil water (enough to cover all ingredients evenly)
- 1 tbsp red pesto (optional)
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 3-4 bay leaves

## Method

1. In an oven proof dish heat the olive oil and add the beef pieces and sear until brown, then remove and set aside.

2. In the same pan add onions and leek and a knob of butter and sauté until soft for 10-12 mins.

3. Then add the mushrooms and sauté for a further 5-10 mins.

4. Now add back in the beef and then the carrots. Next add the flour and dried seasoning, then the tomato puree, beef stock and red wine. Mix well and allow the wine to simmer for 3-4 mins.

5. Now add water to fully submerge all ingredients. Stir gently and ensure everything is covered well.

6. Now add the red pesto (optional), Worcestershire sauce and the bay leaves.

7. Cover and cook slowly in the oven for 2-2.5 hours at 170°C until your beef is tender. Stir every 30 mins and add small amounts of water in each stir if necessary.

8. If you wish to make cottage pie: Allow beef mix to cool then top it with your potato and some Parmesan cheese. Bake in the oven for a further 25 mins at 210°C.
