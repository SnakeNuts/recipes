# Gnocchi alla Sorrentina

## Ingredients

### Assembly
- Gnocchi (bought or homemade: see below)
- Tomato sauce (bought or homemade: see below)
- Parmigiano reggiano
- 300g mozzarella

### Gnocchi
- 1.5kg red potatoes
- 275-350g 00 flour
- 1 egg

### Sauce
- 1-1.5L tomato passata
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 big onion
- 4 garlic cloves
- Basil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Olive oil

## Method

### Gnocchi
1. Make holes in the potatoes with a fork. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in the oven at 200°C until soft.

2. Rice the potatoes. On a clean surface make a volcano from the potatoes and add the flour and egg to the hole.

3. Knead until combined (make sure not to overknead).

4. Bring a generous pot with water and salt to a boil. Boil the gnocchi until they float.

### Sauce
1. Mince the onion and garlic.

2. Sautee the onion and garlic in an ovensafe pan with olive oil for a few minutes.

3. Add the tomato paste and fry for another minute before going in with the passata, some basil, salt and pepper.

4. Optionally add the parmesan rind and let simmer until the oil comes to the sides when you make a well with a spoon.

### Assembly
1. Mix the gnocchi with the sauce, top with the mozzarella and parmigiano and bake until golden and melted in the oven.

2. Serve and enjoy.
