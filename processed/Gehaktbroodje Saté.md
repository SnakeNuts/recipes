# Gehaktbroodje Saté

Meatball Satay Roll - The perfect hangover cure.

## Ingredients

- 1 French baguette
- Cream/milk
- 300-350g mixed pork and beef mince
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 3 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
- 2 tsp sambal oelek
- 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
- 1 garlic clove, grated
- 1 spring onion, finely sliced
- Young Gouda cheese

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.

2. Cut the ends off the baguette. Cut in half lengthways and halve each piece. Scrape out the inside of the bread. Let it soak in some cream or milk.

3. Mix the mince together with the peanut butter, kecap manis, sambal, ginger, garlic and spring onion. Squeeze out the bread and add it. Mix well.

4. Fill the hollowed-out bread pieces with this mixture. Shave slices of cheese and distribute over the rolls.

5. Put in the oven for approximately 20-25 minutes until cooked through.
