# Comforting Cottage Pie

## Ingredients

### Braised Beef (6 portions)
- 1.5kg beef cheek
- 375ml red wine (full bodied)
- 300ml beef stock
- 2 carrots
- 2 celery sticks
- 1 onion
- 1/2 garlic bulb
- 30g tomato paste
- 1 bouquet garni
- Salt + pepper

### Hachis Parmentier (6 portions)
- Braised beef (from above)
- 2 onions
- 4 garlic cloves
- 130g beurre manie (65g butter / 65g flour)
- 10g parsley, chopped
- Salt + pepper

### Potato Topping
- 3 potatoes
- 75g butter
- 30ml whole milk
- Nutmeg, grated
- Grated Emmental cheese
- Salt

## Method

### Braised Beef
1. Start by colouring the beef cheeks, followed by the veg. Once the veg is coloured add the tomato paste and cook for a couple of minutes.

2. Slowly add the red wine to make a sauce, add some beef stock, check seasoning and add back all your coloured meat and veg.

3. Cook at 140°C for approx. 2-3 hours or until the beef cheeks are very tender.

### Hachis Parmentier
1. Sweat the onions for 5-10 minutes and then add the chopped garlic and cook for another 5 minutes.

2. Add the shredded meat, red wine/stock, and thicken with the beurre manie.

3. Finish with the parsley and seasoning and place in a roasting dish.

4. Bake the potatoes at 180°C for one hour. Scoop out the cooked flesh and put through a sieve or ricer. Add the butter and milk and mix well, check seasoning.

5. Put the potato on top of the beef mix and bake at 220°C for 20 minutes or until the top is golden brown and crisp.
