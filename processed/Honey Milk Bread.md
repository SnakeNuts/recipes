# Honey Milk Bread

The easiest, softest, most tender bread ever!

## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 7g instant yeast
- 57g honey
- 43g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
- 165g warm whole milk
- 60g warm water
- 9g salt
- 410g bread flour
- Optional: egg wash (1 egg and a splash of water)

## Method

1. Warm the milk to lukewarm. Add your yeast and mix together all wet ingredients in a bowl. Whisk the egg before combining.

2. Add the bread flour gradually and mix until a soft dough forms. Rest the dough (covered) for about 15 minutes.

3. Then continue kneading until the gluten has started to develop and the dough comes together.

4. Add salt and knead for 2-3 more minutes till the dough passes a light windowpane test. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.

5. Gently shape into a loaf. Place into a buttered 23x13cm loaf pan. Cover and let rise until puffy but still slightly springy when pressed, about 20-30 minutes.

6. Preheat oven to 175°C. Whisk the egg wash ingredients together and brush lightly over the loaf.

7. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then remove and cool completely before slicing for best structure.
