# Slow Cooker Beef Rendang

Serves 4.

## Ingredients

- 1 onion finely diced
- 1 tbsp garlic and ginger paste
- 1 heaped tbsp tomato puree
- 1 heaped tbsp lemongrass paste
- 1 heaped tbsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chilli flakes
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 beef stockpot
- 3 tbsp kecap manis
- 60g desiccated coconut toasted
- 1 x 400g tin coconut milk
- 800g beef cheek cubed (approx. 2 cheeks)
- Juice 1 lime
- 1 tbsp sugar

## Method

1. Pop all of the ingredients apart from the lime juice and sugar into your slow cooker pot then cook on the high setting for four hours or the low setting for eight.

2. To finish the curry add the lime juice and sugar to taste then serve with a portion of rice.
