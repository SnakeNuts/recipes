# Cheesy Chicken Crunch Pockets

## Ingredients

- 3 large flour tortillas
- 280g chicken breast or thighs, finely chopped or small dice
- 1 tsp butter or olive oil
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
- 120ml corn (canned or frozen, thawed)
- 80ml heavy cream
- 196g shredded mozzarella (or cheddar blend)
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Optional: chili flakes
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

## Method

1. Heat a pan over medium heat. Add butter (or olive oil) and the chicken. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink.

2. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until soft.

3. Add bell peppers, corn, salt, pepper, paprika, chili flakes, and garlic powder. Cook until the vegetables soften and everything smells fragrant.

4. Pour in the cream (+ optionally some cheese) and simmer for 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened. Remove from heat and let the filling cool slightly.

5. Wet the wrap with room-temperature water to make it flexible. Cut each wrap in half.

6. Spoon the chicken filling onto one side of each piece. Add shredded cheese on top.

7. Fold into tight triangles (or pockets).

8. Arrange on a lined baking tray (grease the pan with oil optionally). Sprinkle a little extra cheese on top if desired.

9. Bake in a preheated oven at 175-180°C for 20-25 minutes, until deeply golden and crispy.

10. Let rest for 5 minutes, then enjoy that crunch.
