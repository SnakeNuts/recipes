# Oliebollen

Dutch Doughnuts.

## Ingredients

- 500g flour
- 500ml lukewarm milk
- 1 egg
- 14g yeast
- 7g salt
- 3 tbsp light brown custard sugar
- 100g currants
- 150g raisins

## Method

1. Mix the yeast with lukewarm milk and set aside.

2. In a large mixing bowl mix together the flour, salt, and sugar.

3. Create a well in the flour mixture and go in with the milk and an egg. Mix well so that there are no flour clumps.

4. Soak some raisins in warm water.

5. Mix the currants and raisins through the dough.

6. Cover the mixing bowl with plastic wrap and let rise for 1 hour.

7. Heat a pan with oil to 180°C.

8. Take two spoons and dip them in the oil before dropping golf ball size balls of batter into the oil. Fry for around 6-8 minutes.

9. Serve with icing sugar and enjoy.
