# Tuscan Chicken Loaded Potatoes

Creamy Tuscan chicken with sundried tomatoes, spinach and basil on top of crispy cubed potatoes all made in under 20 minutes.

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 300g chicken breast
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- 1 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
- 1 tsp garlic granules
- 3 tsp paprika
- Pinch salt and pepper
- 10g plain flour

### Potatoes
- 400g potatoes, diced into small cubes
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tsp paprika

### Sauce
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 40g sundried tomatoes, sliced
- 50g single cream or alternative
- 100-120ml chicken stock
- 5g Parmesan cheese, grated
- A handful of spinach
- Fresh basil

## Method

1. Butterfly the chicken breast then slice all the way through. Season with salt, pepper, 1 tsp mixed herbs, 1 tsp paprika and garlic granules. Coat in the flour. Spray the crisp tray with low calorie spray and air fry on 190°C for 7 mins. Flip and cook for a further 6 mins.

2. For the potatoes season generously with salt, pepper and 1 tsp paprika. Air fry on 190°C for 15 mins shaking every 5 mins.

3. For the sauce fry the onions with low calorie spray or sundried tomato oil for a few mins to soften. Add the sundried tomatoes with the minced garlic for a minute then mix in 100ml chicken stock, single cream and grated parmesan. Simmer until it starts to thicken then mix in the spinach. Slice the chicken then mix that in. Add the rest of the stock to loosen the sauce if needed.

4. Enjoy with fresh basil.
