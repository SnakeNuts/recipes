# Berenklauw

A meatball and onion dish that is fantastic, or as we say berelekker!

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 500g beef mince
- 75g bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 onion
- 1/4 bunch parsley
- 1 tsp sambal oelek
- 1 tsp mustard
- Splash ketjap manis
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika

### Peanut Sauce
- 400g peanuts
- 400g coconut milk
- 2 shallots/onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 cm ginger
- 1 chili pepper
- 50-100ml ketjap manis
- 1-2 tsp sambal oelek
- 1-2 lime(s)
- 1-2 tbsp palm sugar or brown sugar
- Optional: laos, coriander

## Method

### Meatballs
1. Mince the onion, chop the parsley and mix with the beef, bread crumbs, egg, sambal, mustard, kecap, salt and pepper.

2. Form balls from the beef and optional let it stiffen up in the fridge.

3. Cook the meatballs in a pan with butter until almost fully/fully cooked.

4. Slice some big onions into very thick rings.

5. Slice the meatballs in three equal pieces and put onto a skewer with the onion rings.

6. Fry for 2-3 minutes at 170°C.

7. Serve with the peanut sauce and fried onions.

### Peanut Sauce
1. Roast the peanuts with the shallot, garlic, ginger and chili pepper.

2. Add them to a blender with half the coconut milk and blend until smooth.

3. Add the sauce to a pan with oil and bake for a few minutes, before stirring in the kecap and the other half of the coconut milk, season to taste with salt and pepper and lime juice.
