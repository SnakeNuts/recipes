# Satay Ayam

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 900g chicken thighs
- 4-6 shallots
- 8 garlic cloves
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp chicken powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp cilantro
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp oil
- 60-90ml kecap manis

### Peanut Sauce
- 500g peanuts
- 3 shallots
- 4 garlic cloves
- 2 candlenuts
- 6 kaffir lime leaves
- 3 small chili peppers
- 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 lime juice
- 3 tbsp palm sugar
- 60ml kecap manis

### Glaze
- 1:1 peanut sauce:kecap manis

## Method

### Chicken
1. Blend the shallots, garlic, spices, kecap manis and oil to a smooth paste.

2. Slice the chicken into small pieces and marinate with the paste.

3. Mix some of the peanut sauce with more kecap manis to make a glaze for the chicken.

4. Put the chicken onto skewers and grill until charred, brushing a few times with the glaze.

### Peanut Sauce
1. Heat the peanuts with shallots, garlic, chilies and candle nuts until some oil releases.

2. Blend until smooth adding some water if needed.

3. Mix the paste with sugar, tamarind, kecap manis, lime juice and kaffir lime and let simmer for a little.
