# Hachee

Recipe for 4 servings.

## Ingredients

- 800g beef rib or beef chuck
- Salt and pepper
- 3 tbsp plain flour
- 2 onions
- 25ml sunflower oil
- 25g butter
- 1 tsp paprika powder
- 3 bay leaves
- 6 cloves
- 25ml red wine vinegar
- 500ml beef stock
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp apple syrup or molasses

## Method

1. Cut the meat into 3 x 3 cm cubes, season with salt and pepper and dust with flour.

2. Cut the onions into pieces.

3. Brown the meat on all sides in oil and butter.

4. Add the onions and let them brown briefly together.

5. Add paprika powder, bay leaves and cloves and deglaze with vinegar, stock, soy sauce and syrup.

6. Let the hachee simmer gently with the lid on the pan for 3 hours. Remove the lid for the last half hour so the gravy can reduce slightly.

7. Check the flavour and remove any bay leaves and cloves if you can find them.
