# Chicken Massaman Noodles

Usually served with rice, but adding in noodles to this one pot wonder is a game changer.

## Ingredients

- 1 pack boneless chicken thighs
- 2 tins coconut milk
- Big dash of light and dark soy
- Juice of 1 lime
- Dash of fish sauce
- Big blob of massaman curry paste
- Your favourite veggies (red pepper, red onion, bean sprouts, green beans)
- Cornflour slurry (optional, for thickening)
- Your favourite noodles
- Lime wedges for serving
- Fresh coriander for serving

## Method

1. Add whole pack of boneless chicken thighs to a pot.

2. Add in 2 tins of coconut milk, big dash of light and dark soy, juice of 1 lime, dash of fish sauce, and a big blob of massaman curry paste.

3. Chuck in your favourite veggies (red pepper, red onion, bean sprouts, green beans).

4. Cover the pot and add to oven on 150-160°C for 2-3 hours until the chicken is cooked and falling apart.

5. Once removed, you may need to add a cornflour slurry to thicken the sauce to your liking.

6. At this point, add in your favourite noodles and pop back in the oven for 10 mins to cook and absorb all that lovely sauce.

7. Serve with a wedge of lime and fresh coriander.
