# Sheet-Pan Beef Kebabs

Juicy, high-protein, weeknight-easy. No skewers. No fuss.

## Ingredients

### Kebabs
- 908g ground beef (85/15)
- 60ml parsley, finely chopped
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 3/4 tsp black pepper
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp honey or date syrup (optional)

### Yogurt Sauce
- 180ml greek yogurt
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt + pepper
- 1 1/2-2 tbsp finely chopped dill
- Optional parsley

### Pita Build (optional)
- Yogurt sauce
- Beef
- Tomato + cucumber (pinch salt + olive oil)
- Pickled red onion
- Chili oil

## Method

### Kebabs
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Mix everything gently until just combined.

2. Press onto a sheet pan (about 3/4" thick).

3. Score into kebabs.

4. Bake about 12-14 min until just cooked. Spoon off excess fat.

5. Broil 2-3 min until browned.

### Yogurt Sauce
1. Mix greek yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, honey, salt, pepper and dill together.

2. Optional add parsley.

### Pita Build
1. Yogurt sauce, beef, tomato and cucumber (with pinch salt and olive oil), pickled red onion and chili oil.
