# Cheese Crackers

3 ingredient easy homemade cheese crackers. They cost just over 1 euro for the whole lot and can last up to a week if stored in an airtight container.

## Ingredients

- 175g cheddar cheese grated
- 120g plain flour
- 56g room temp butter
- Optional seasoning - pinch of salt (sometimes add rosemary)
- 1-5 tbsp water (enough to bring together)

## Method

1. Add grated cheese, flour, butter and seasoning if using to a blender and blend until mixed.

2. Add in 1 tbsp water at a time and blend until the mixture comes together to form a dough.

3. Cover and place the dough into the fridge for 30 mins.

4. Preheat the oven to 180°C.

5. Roll the dough out on a floured surface until it is about 3-4mm thick and cut into cracker shapes. You can use a mini cookie cutter or even just simple squares.

6. Place evenly on parchment paper on a tray and cook for 10 mins.

7. Allow to cool on the tray fully before transferring to an airtight container.
