# Cheesy BBQ Chicken Pasta

Serves 4.

## Ingredients

- 300g pasta (uncooked weight)
- 500g chicken, diced
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tbsp oregano
- 1/2 tbsp garlic granules
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 2 peppers, finely diced
- 2 tsp garlic puree (or 2 cloves)
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- 4 tbsp bbq sauce
- 300ml chicken stock
- 120g cheese (cheddar and mozzarella mix)
- Bbq sauce, chilli flakes and parsley for garnish

## Method

1. Pop your pasta onto boil with plenty of salt.

2. Meanwhile fry off the chicken with the seasonings until browned, then add in the onions and peppers and cook until soft.

3. Once soft add in the garlic and cook for 30 seconds.

4. Then add in the tomato puree and bbq sauce and cook for another minute or so.

5. Add in the chicken stock and cook until it's reduced to form a thick sauce.

6. Add in the cooked pasta and a little pasta water.

7. Sprinkle with cheese and pop a lid on or put under the grill until golden and bubbling.

8. Garnish and serve.
