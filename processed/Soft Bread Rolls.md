# Soft Bread Rolls

## Ingredients

- 250g lukewarm water
- 7g instant yeast
- Squeeze of honey (about 1 tbsp)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 350g strong bread flour
- 1/2 tbsp salt

## Method

1. Mix the water and yeast and add the honey and olive oil and mix again. Add flour and salt and mix well until combined and sticky.

2. Cover and rest 30 min.

3. After 30 min do 4 stretch and folds with a damp hand to prevent the dough from sticking. Reaching round and underneath the dough lift it up gently and fold over. Turn the bowl after each fold. Cover and rest 30 min.

4. After 30 min repeat the process and cover and rest in the fridge for 10-24 hours. You can add some oil to your cling film to stop it sticking but if it does just pull the dough away from the plastic.

5. Once ready prepare your dish (23cm square) with parchment paper and some flour. Flour the top of dough and the work surface generously and turn the bowl out on top of the baking dish. It may take a few minutes and if you don't have the patience you can help with your hand.

6. With floured hands stretch the dough slightly from underneath, sprinkle the top with more flour and cut into 9. Do strong cuts into the dough until you feel the dish at the bottom and if it becomes sticky add more flour. Cover and rest for 30 min while your oven heats up.

7. Heat your oven to 210°C and place a little oven dish with some water inside (300ml) at the bottom of your oven as soon as your turn it on.

8. Bake for 15 min, turn oven down to 190°C and take out the dish with water. Bake for another 15 min at 190°C.

9. Take out of the dish and cool for at least 20 min before eating. When they come out they'll feel hard but will become soft as they cool.
