# Chicken Tikka Rice Bake

## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts, chopped
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 3 tbsp ground almond
- 1 tbsp lazy garlic
- 220g basmati rice
- 2–3 handfuls of mixed vegetables
- 159g cherry tomatoes
- 300ml coconut milk
- 400ml chicken stock
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 4 whole cardamom pods (optional)

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients apart from cheese, add that last.
2. Cover with foil.
3. Bake at 190°C for 45 minutes.
4. Remove foil and bake for a further 10 minutes to brown.
