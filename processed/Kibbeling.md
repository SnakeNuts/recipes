# Kibbeling

## Ingredients

### Fish Batter
- 800g cod fillet
- 350g flour
- 300ml beer
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp pepper
- Lots of fish spices

### Fish Spices
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp mustard powder
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- 1 tsp dill
- 1 tsp cumin

### Tartare Sauce
- 150g mayonnaise
- 100g creme fraiche
- 2 tbsp capers
- 4 small pickles
- 1 tsp mustard
- 1 tsp fish spices
- Dill
- Chives
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Method

1. Slice the cod filets in chunks.

2. Mix together the flour, salt, fish spices, beer and an egg.

3. Coat the cod chunks in the batter and set aside.

4. Make the fish spices by mixing all the spices listed.

5. For the sauce chop some pickles, capers, dill, chives and parsley.

6. Combine the mayo with creme fraiche, mustard, lemon juice, salt, pepper and fish spices before adding in the chopped pickles, capers, dill, chives and parsley.

7. Fry the cod in neutral oil at 180°C until golden in about 3-5 minutes.

8. Serve the kibbeling, sprinkle with the fish spices and enjoy with a wedge of lemon and the sauce.
