# Butter Chicken

A perfect dish to meal prep for the rest of the week. Simply heat it up and serve with cumin yellow rice and a fresh side salad.

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 800g chicken
- 150g yoghurt
- Lemon juice
- 2 cm ginger
- 4 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp coriander
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tsp fenugreek
- Pinch of salt
- 1 tbsp oil

### Sauce
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Cinnamon stick
- 6 cardamom pods
- 4 cloves
- 2 onions
- 1kg tomatoes
- 3 cm ginger
- 5 garlic cloves
- 2 green chilies
- 75g cashews
- 3 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 3 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp fenugreek
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 100ml heavy cream
- 480ml chicken stock
- 2 tbsp butter or ghee

### Naan
- 400g flour
- 90ml water
- 190g Greek yoghurt
- 1 tsp yeast
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp salt

### Rice
- 2 tbsp ghee
- Cumin seeds
- Basmati rice
- Turmeric powder
- Water

### Salad
- Cabbage
- Red onion
- Orange
- Salt
- Pepper

## Method

1. Marinate the chicken with all the chicken ingredients.

2. Make the naan by kneading all the naan ingredients together. Cover and let double in size.

3. For the sauce fry down the aromatics with ghee. Remove and sauté all the other veggies with the spices except tomatoes.

4. Add tomatoes and cashews, let simmer until soft.

5. Grill the chicken in the oven. Remove and add the juice together with some stock to the sauce. Blend until smooth.

6. Finish the sauce with the chicken, ghee and heavy cream.

7. Form balls from the naan dough, roll out and bake in a dry pan until golden and soft.

8. For the rice fry down the cumin seeds in ghee. Add the rice and toast before adding water. When done, mix with a mixture of turmeric and water.

9. Chop the cabbage into strips and make half moons from the red onion.

10. For the salad mix the chopped cabbage with red onion, salt, pepper, orange juice and zest.

11. Serve and garnish with coriander and a splash of heavy cream. (Reheating the naan breads: simply brush/spray with water and bake again in a dry skillet until soft and fluffy)
