# Broodje Grillworst

## Ingredients

### Grillworst
- 800g chicken thighs
- Spice mixture to taste (below)
- Cornstarch
- Old mature cheese
- Bread rolls

### Joppiesaus
- 6 tbsp mayo
- 1 shallot
- 1 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp curry sauce
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp curry
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 1/2 tsp turmeric

### Spice Mixture
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1/2-1 tsp mace or 1/2 tsp nutmeg

## Method

### Grillworst
1. Finely chop the chicken thighs.

2. Mix with the spices and cornstarch.

3. Spread out on plastic wrap and layer some cheese in the middle. Roll to a sausage and wrap tight.

4. Simmer in a pan with water for 30-40 minutes (don't boil).

5. Brush with butter and cover with the same spices.

6. Grill in the oven for 10-15 minutes.

### Joppiesaus
1. Mince the shallot.

2. Mix everything together.
