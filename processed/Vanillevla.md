# Vanillevla

Vanilla Custard is really easy to make yourself!

Makes about 1 litre.

## Ingredients

- 1 vanilla pod
- 1 litre full-fat milk
- Pinch of salt
- 4 egg yolks
- 100g sugar
- 40g custard powder or cornstarch

## Method

1. Cut the vanilla pod lengthwise in half and scrape out the vanilla pulp.

2. Warm the milk with vanilla and salt and reserve a little milk.

3. Stir together a paste of egg yolks, sugar, custard powder and cold milk.

4. Slowly pour the warm milk into the paste while stirring and pour back into the pan.

5. Let simmer very gently for 5 minutes, keep stirring gently and then let cool completely and chill in the fridge.

Note: Keeps well sealed for a week in the fridge.
