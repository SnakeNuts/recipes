# Oven-Baked Chicken Shawarma

The chicken comes out so juicy, so flavorful, and it makes for easy meals all week long.

## Ingredients

- 1.1kg boneless chicken thighs
- 2-3 tbsp olive oil
- 3-4 pinches of salt
- Fresh cracked pepper
- 2 tbsp plain yogurt
- Juice of 1/2 large lemon
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 1.5 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tbsp Aleppo pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp cardamom
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 large red onion, thinly sliced

## Method

1. Start with 1.1kg of boneless chicken thighs. Trim off any excess fat to the extent you'd like. Add it to a bowl.

2. Season the chicken well. Add the olive oil, salt, pepper, yogurt and lemon juice. Then add all the spices. Mix it super well.

3. Thinly slice 1/2 large red onion and mix it in with the chicken. Ideally let this marinate for 2-6 hours, but even better overnight, but if you want to make it right away, you can.

4. When you're ready to make it, remove the chicken from the fridge and let it come up to room temp (about 30 mins). Preheat your oven to 220°C then pack the marinated chicken tightly into a bread loaf pan.

5. Pop it into the oven for 45-55 mins at 220°C.

6. Remove the chicken from the oven, tilt the pan over a glass bowl or container to pour out the excess juices (save the juice to use as broth or to pour over the chicken).

7. Flip the loaf pan onto a cutting board to remove the chicken and cut it into thin slices or strips.

8. Serve it up however way you want - as is, on top of a salad, on a power bowl, whatever! This stays well in the fridge in an airtight lid for 4-5 days.
