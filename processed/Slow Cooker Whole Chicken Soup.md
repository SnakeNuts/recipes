# Slow Cooker Whole Chicken Soup

Makes 4-6 servings (suitable for freezing).

## Ingredients

### For Slow Cooking
- 1 whole chicken (approximately 1.2kg)
- Pinch of salt
- 1 lemon, halved
- 2 bay leaves, or other hard herbs (thyme/rosemary)
- Small handful peppercorns

### For Later
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 carrots, roughly diced
- 1 large onion (or 2 small), diced
- 2 celery sticks, diced
- 5 garlic cloves, minced
- Salt/pepper, to taste
- Optional: chicken powder/soup flavouring
- Large handful fresh parsley, chopped

## Method

1. Sprinkle your chicken with salt and place breast side down in the slow cooker. Add a halved lemon. Add bay leaves, peppercorns or other aromats. Cover, or nearly cover, with water. Leave an inch at the top of the slow cooker to avoid overflowing. Cook for 7+ hours on low. You can leave it all day.

2. After the slow cook, transfer the chicken to a tray and keep the liquid. Keep the lemon but discard herbs/peppercorns. Remove the chicken skin and bones. Shred the meat, set aside and cover to keep warm.

3. In a large pot, sauté onions, celery and carrots. After a few mins, turn down the heat, add garlic and cook a further 2 mins. Pour in your slow cooker broth. Season with salt and pepper, or add flavourings. Squeeze in some leftover lemon.

4. Simmer until the veggies are soft enough to be blitzed with a hand mixer. Be very careful with hot liquid. You can let it cool first. Remember: never put hot liquid into a sealed blender.

5. Add the shredded chicken to your soup and heat everything through. Season further if needed and add soft herbs like parsley before serving.
