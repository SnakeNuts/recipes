# Bangers and Mash Bake

Easy, simple, delicious and affordable baked meatballs and onion gravy topped with cheese.

## Ingredients

- 3 medium onions or 2 large ones
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 480ml beef stock
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp mustard
- Salt and generous crackings of pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp cornflour (+ 1 tbsp water)
- 120ml cheddar, grated
- Creamy mashed potatoes
- Pork bangers

## Method

1. In a pot, fry onions on medium heat until caramelised.

2. Throw in the thyme and rosemary and pour in the beef stock.

3. Add the Worcestershire sauce, mustard and salt to taste and simmer for 20 minutes.

4. Make a slurry using the cornstarch and water and pour into the gravy.

5. Stir until thickened and simmer for a further 3 minutes.

6. In an oven-safe dish, layer the mash first, add the pork bangers (you can slice the bangers lengthwise if you are using a shallow dish to get more space).

7. Drizzle some onion gravy and spread the mash on top, covering all the gravy.

8. Sprinkle some cheese.

9. Bake at 180°C for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

10. Serve immediately with some of the leftover gravy and some greens.
