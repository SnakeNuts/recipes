# Dutch Meatballs with Gravy

Also known as Oma's Meatball.

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 500g mixed pork and beef mince
- 2 pieces white bread
- 50-100ml milk
- 3 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
- Paprika powder
- Coriander seeds crushed
- Onion powder
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Curcuma
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp flour

### Gravy
- 150g butter
- 300ml beef stock
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp mustard
- Parsley
- Rosemary
- Splash of kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)

## Method

1. To a bowl add the white bread with milk and let it soak.

2. In a bowl mix together the soaked bread, mince, kecap manis, Dutch meatball spices, salt and pepper.

3. If the mixture is still wet add some breadcrumbs to it.

4. Make four to five equal balls and coat them with flour.

5. Melt the butter in a big pan and let it warm. Add the balls and sear on all sides.

6. Go in with the beef stock, tomato paste, some kecap manis, rosemary sprigs and mustard. Let it simmer on low for 20-30 minutes.

7. Chop some parsley.

8. Serve with white bread, the gravy and garnish with parsley.
