# Rodekool met Appeltjes

Red Cabbage with Apples.

Recipe for 4 servings. Instead of shredding the cabbage yourself, you can also buy it pre-shredded from the greengrocer.

## Ingredients

- 500g finely shredded red cabbage
- 1 red onion, chopped
- 2 apples (jonagold or goudrenette), chopped
- 30g butter
- 2 juniper berries, crushed
- 2 cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 200ml water
- 200ml berry juice or redcurrant juice
- 1 tbsp cornstarch

## Method

1. Fry the onion briefly in butter.

2. Add all the ingredients except the cornstarch to the pan and bring to the boil.

3. Turn the heat low and let the red cabbage cook gently with the lid on the pan for 45 minutes until tender.

4. Taste the red cabbage to check it's cooked and tasty.

5. Remove any bay leaves, cinnamon stick and other spices if you can find them.

6. Mix cornstarch with a splash of cold water and stir this into the gently simmering red cabbage.

7. Let it simmer gently for another 2 minutes so the starch can cook through.

Note: The cornstarch thickens the cooking liquid so the red cabbage gets a nice gloss and the flavour sits better on your tongue. Homemade red cabbage keeps well for a few days covered in the fridge and can be reheated on low heat in a pan or in the microwave.
